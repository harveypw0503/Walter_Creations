What it does

Gradient Maker is a full-featured gradient maker and editor. Build linear, radial, reflected, angular, and diamond gradients with unlimited color and opacity stops. Bend the angle, position, and scale to your will. There is a library of templates to get inspired with. Save your work as a .wcgr file to reload later and continue editing or to use as a color in other tools on the site by turning on Advanced tooling in the Settings. Export you finished gradients as PNG at any resolution.

How to use
1
Click on ＋ Color Stop or ◇ Opacity Stop to add stops or click above or below the gradient bar to add color and opacity stops. Drag stops along the bar to reposition them.
2
Change canvas ratio to your desired proportions.
3
Feel free to use the Template Library and try various Randomize functions for inspiration, fun, or to discover complimenting colors.
4
Your past 10 edits are saved in the Edit History where you can simply go back to any previous step by clicking on it.
5
Save your creation as a PNG if so desired or as a WCGR so you can edit it later and use it in other tools via Advanced Tooling.
💡 The ratio does not have any bearing on PNG export size, only the PNG proportions.
