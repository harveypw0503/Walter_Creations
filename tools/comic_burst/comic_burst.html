What it does

The Comic Burst Generator generates starburst and explosion shapes similar to classic comic books and pop art. You have full control over length, color, spike count, randomness and other factors. Export your result as PNG or scalable SVG.

How to use
1
Pick a Burst Style: Organic Burst or Classic Sharp Burst.
2
Choose your colors, set Spike Count and lengths, then toggle the Black Outline on or off to suit your design.
3
Adjust randomness and angle jitter to your liking.
4
Hit Generate to render, then Download PNG or Download SVG to save your burst.
💡 By setting randomness and angle jitter all the way down, you can replicate the Classic Sharp Burst in the Organic Burst preset.
