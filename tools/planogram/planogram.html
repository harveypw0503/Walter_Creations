What it does

The Planogram Generator creates visual mockup diagrams for pegboard and slatwall displays. Input your SKUs, Product Name or description and Width by Height; then and the tool automatically arranges them on a to-scale board. Export your result as PNG, TXT, or CSV.

How to use
1
Enter your Pegboard size in holes. (Each hole is designed to signify an inch but can be any unit of measurement).
2
List your Items, one per line using the format SKU|Description|WxH where W and H are the item's footprint in holes. Be sure to use 'x' to in dimensions and vertical bars in between sections
3
Choose your Board Style: (Pegboard or Slatwall), Packing Mode: (Compact or Row), and Customize spacing between bags with the spacing controls.
4
Click Generate to render the mockup, then click Download PNG to save an image of the mockup. TXT and CSV are also available for download.
💡 There are multiple ways to easily get vertical bars, (this character: "|"), in your input for the Planogram Generator. You can use an Excel formula like =A1&"|"&B1&"|"&C1 or =TEXTJOIN("|", TRUE, A1:C1) to concatenate columns with vertical bars automatically.
