What it does

The Mountain Range Generator generates mountain ridge silhouettes. Every generation is unique. You can control the canvas size, the jaggedness of the peaks are, height of the mountains, and the colors of both the range and the sky. Export your result as PNG or scalable SVG.

How to use
1
Set the Width and Height of the canvas in pixels.
2
Select your Mountain Color and Sky Color.
3
Adjust Jaggedness to control the variation in mountain height.
4
Adjust Mountain Height to control how much of the canvas the range fills vertically.
5
Click Generate to form, the Download PNG or Download SVG to save your range.
💡 In SVG exports, the sky is a rectangular shape with a portion under the mountain range layer; it is NOT cut where the range and sky meet.
