What it does

The Sunray Generator generates radiating ray patterns in center, up, down, left and right directions. You have full control over the ray count, colors, lengths and the direction. Export your result as PNG or a scalable SVG.

How to use
1
Choose a Direction: Center radiates from the middle; Up/Down/Left/Right shifts the origin to that edge.
2
Set Number of Rays and pick a Ray Color using the color picker.
3
Adjust Min Length & Max Length to control how short and long the rays can be. Check Fixed Length to make all the same size.
4
Hit Generate to create a new random arrangement, then Download PNG or Download SVG to save your rays.
💡 If working with white or black and gray colors, change the day/night mode toggle to see your creation.
