About Walter Creations

Walter Creations is an idea born from imagination, curiosity, and the desire to create and innovate. Created in 2025 by our founder Harvey Walter, we offer a few unique online tools that are completely free to use as we strive to empower every creator and individual.

One of our first physical creations and services we offer is custom made 3D printed lithopanes. These are magnificent works of art that reveal intricate images when light passes through them. Each lithopane undergoes a quality check before reaching your hands.
